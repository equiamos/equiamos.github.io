The Great American Roadtrip

I'm a big fan of roadtrips. Over the past few years I've spent nearly a year on the road, over the course of many cross-country trips, spanning a cumulative total of over 50,000 miles. Thought I would take this opportunity to share some recommendations from my time On The Road.

Caveat

Obviously, interests differ, and the nature and goals of your trip will dictate the applicability of my suggestions. For instance, although I've visited quite a number of cities during the course of my travels, overall I've spent relatively little time in them, favoring national parks and scenic roads. My trips were geared toward the adventurous 20-something on a shoe-string budget attempting to satiate severe wanderlust. Nearly every night was spent camping or sleeping in the car, so accommodations were never a concern. I'll do my best to specify considerations that come to mind for individual suggestions.
